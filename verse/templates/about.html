What Inspired the development of Verse-Connect?

Verse-Connect is not a project that I decided to venture on lately rather it’s a dream I’ve had of making a reality long before I joined alx When growing up I’ve thought of how things could be done more swiftly and conveniently.
It’s true we already have platforms that allow for shopping and access of services but to be they are quite limited and only favour the big companies
which have the resources to maintain such platforms. Our country is made of me a and you as well, we want a way we can access services from each other and render our services to more people at convenient prices and time.We need a way to balance
between the areas of plenty and the areas of demand. I thought about it all and thought something has to be done. An good example is of M-pesa. Before M-pesa hit the ground running in kenya people used to rely on banks and had long queues to get cash or even deposit their money. The banking system at the time was the major voice of the cashflow system in kenya. When mpesa came it did not only close the queues and made it convenient for the people
of kenya but also gave them the power to control their funds as they wish. They can send receive and withdraw at the time of their convenience. Today it’s very hard to imagine life without mpesa in kenya. Both big and small businesses have implemented and are using mpesa services. I don’t mean to dive deeper into mpesa yet the truth of the matter is Verse-Connect is not just a pass and go concept project. It’s holds the power to change many lives in our country and even the continent of Africa if well implemented.
